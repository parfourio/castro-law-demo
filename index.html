About Our Firm

A Family Firm Built for Families

Castro Law Offices, P.C. is located in Novato, California and dedicated exclusively to family law. Founded by attorney Jeremy Castro, our firm was built on a simple belief: families going through difficult legal situations deserve skilled, compassionate, and honest representation.

As a veteran-owned and family-owned business, we understand what's truly at stake — your children, your home, your future. We don't just litigate cases. We guide families through some of the hardest chapters of their lives with integrity and purpose.

Our multilingual team speaks English, Spanish, and Portuguese, ensuring that every client in Marin County and beyond receives counsel in the language they're most comfortable with.

Areas We Serve

Marin County: Novato, San Rafael, Greenbrae, San Anselmo, Fairfax, Sausalito, Larkspur, Mill Valley, Corte Madera, Ross, West Marin — plus Sonoma, Napa, Solano, San Francisco, and Alameda Counties.

Family Law Practice Areas

Exclusively focused on family law, we handle the full spectrum of issues affecting California families.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Law & Parentage

Legal parent-child relationships, court recognition, and parental rights and obligations.

🏠

Child Custody

Joint or sole custody arrangements, educational and medical decision-making rights.

💍

Divorce Law

Experienced Marin County divorce representation to protect your interests throughout proceedings.

🧬

Paternity Law

California parentage laws, genetic testing, and court-order representation for all parties.

💰

Spousal Support

Spousal and domestic support orders following the dissolution of marriage or partnership.

🔄

Order Modifications

Modify existing custody, visitation, or support orders when circumstances change.

🧸

Adoption Law

Guiding families through California adoption proceedings with care and expertise.

🛡️

Domestic Violence & Restraining Orders

Protective orders against spouses, cohabitants, partners, and close relatives.
